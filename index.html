Rules

  • Each Player can roll the dices as many time as they wish until they decide to hold their point and pass the turn or they get a “one”.
  • If a player gets a “one” when they roll the dice, they will not get any points and their turn will be over. A player must have a drink every time they get a 1.
  • If a player roll the dice 6 or more times in a turn and decides to hold his points, He or she gets a flame. If a player gets a streak of 3 flames in a row he will get 50 bonus points and every other player must have a drink.
  • When a player gets a “one” he gets an X. If a player accumulates a streak of 3 "X"s, he or she loses 20 points and must have an extra drink. If the player has 20 or less points his score will be 0.
  • The first Player to reach 250 points wins the game, and every losing player has to have a drink.
